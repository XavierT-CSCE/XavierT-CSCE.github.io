Problem Statement

Basic Statement: While there are many websites that allow for you to vent your frustrations publically, they're usually tied to some sort of identifier like a username. A bit more detailed version: As a person who struggles with mental health issues (anxiety disorders + CPTSD), I've always found it difficult to share how I feel/vent frustrations. While it's possible to make throwaway accounts on website like Twitter/X, that all comes back tied to a certain identifier like a username. Yes, personal applications such as the Notes app does exist, it doesn't allow for someone to give feedback on how to deal with my frustrations. I feel that making some sort of app/website where one can share their frustrations anonymously and recieve feedback on the problem is a good way to help. Joking!
